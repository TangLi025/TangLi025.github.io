Main research interests:

  1. To study the molecular regulatory mechanisms of RNA epigenetic modifications on chromatin structure and gene transcription and the related biological functions in stem cell fate determination, cell reprogramming, anti-tumor immunity and cancer development;
  2. To investigate the molecular regulatory mechanisms of ncRNA degradation processes in the nucleus and related biological functions;
  3. To investigate the molecular regulatory mechanism of RNA epigenetic modification for biomolecular phase transition and the corresponding biological functions.
